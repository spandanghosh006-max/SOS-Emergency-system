    First Aid Guides

    1. Check for responsiveness and breathing.
    2. Call emergency services immediately.
    3. Place hands in the center of the chest.
    4. Push hard and fast (100-120 compressions per minute).
    • Apply direct pressure to the wound with a clean cloth.
    • Maintain pressure until bleeding stops.
    • If necessary, apply a tourniquet above the wound if trained to do so.
    • Cool the burn under cool (not cold) running water for 10-15 minutes.
    • Remove constricting items like rings.
    • Do not apply ice, butter, or ointments.
    • Cover with a sterile, non-fluffy dressing.
